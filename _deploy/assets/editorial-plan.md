# BENZ-ACTION — Plan Éditorial / Editorial Plan (SEO Blog)

Six bilingual article topics targeting stunt & action-design search intent.
Each topic has a French and an English long-tail keyword. When a blog section
is implemented, every article must have its own unique title tag, meta
description, single H1, and canonical URL.

## Keyword strategy summary

**FR primary keywords:** cascadeur cinéma, coordinateur cascades, cascade automobile,
chute de hauteur, combat scénique, sécurité plateau, doublure cascade.

**EN primary keywords:** stunt performer, stunt coordinator, fight choreography, action design.

**Title tag pattern:** `{Article Title} | BENZ-ACTION`
**Meta description:** 150–160 chars, includes the target long-tail keyword once, naturally.
**URL pattern (FR):** `/blog/{slug-fr}.html` — **(EN):** `/en/blog/{slug-en}.html`
**Hreflang:** reciprocal fr/en/x-default per article.
