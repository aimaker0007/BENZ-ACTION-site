👤 About

Professionals of the Extreme

Benz-Action brings together stunt performers and action coordinators trained to Hollywood techniques and international safety standards.

Our Story

The Technical Excellence of the Stunt

Each member brings their own specialty: driving, aerial work, choreographed combat or set safety.

Our reputation is built on reliability: we deliver the impossible on screen, with the certainty that everyone returns safe. From Ouarzazate to Marrakech, we put an intimate knowledge of Moroccan locations at the service of international productions.

🎯 Our Method

Rigor in the Service of Boldness

Every project begins with deep technical analysis. We study your script, assess the risks, and design creative solutions that respect your artistic vision while ensuring total protection for everyone involved.

Our team works across every phase: technical prep on location, safety setup, rehearsals with stunt performers and actors, then supervision during the shoot. We validate every take before the camera rolls.

What defines us:

Technical precision

No improvisation. Every move is calculated, rehearsed, secured.

Premium equipment

Imported gear, certified to international standards.

Local knowledge

Expertise of Moroccan filming locations, from Ouarzazate studios to the alleys of Marrakech.

Iron discipline

Strict safety protocols, constant communication with crews.
